About Brilliant One Education

Brilliant One Education｜锦一教育 is a Canada-based academic tutoring and study planning service designed for students preparing for international education pathways.

We support families with AP, IB, A-Level, IELTS, English speaking, academic competitions, and personalized study planning.

Our goal is not only to help students improve grades, but also to build a clear academic pathway based on each student’s current level, target country, learning pace, and future goals.

Personalized Study Planning｜一对一学习规划

Every student needs a pathway that fits their goals.

Step 1: Understand the student’s grade, current curriculum, strengths, weaknesses, and target country.
Step 2: Design a course plan for AP, IB, A-Level, IELTS, English, or competitions.
Step 3: Match students with suitable tutors and build a weekly learning schedule.
Step 4: Track progress, adjust the study plan, and communicate with parents regularly.
